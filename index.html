The Scando Method: An Agile & Incremental Approach

1. Assessment & Process Discovery

Exhaustive analysis of current operations, workflows, systems (if any), and performance metrics. PCF utilization to categorize and measure digital maturity, establishing KPIs aligned with your strategic goals for incremental improvement.

2. Tailored Odoo ERP Implementation/Enhancement

Leveraging Odoo's modularity, our Business Matter Experts design, implement, or re-engineer and optimize processes in phases, configuring modules and ensuring seamless integration for enhanced usability and functionality.

3. Knowledge Management Integration

Implementing tools and practices within Odoo to document critical processes and decisions, creating a centralized knowledge base to foster collaboration and reduce dependency as your system evolves.

4. Digital Maturity Scoring (Vigor)

Utilizing our proprietary tool, Vigor, to assign scores to each process based on functionality, implementation, business benefit, and user adoption, revealing areas for prioritized, incremental improvements.

5. Training & Change Management

Providing hands-on, role-specific training for each implemented phase and deploying change management strategies to ensure team proficiency and smooth adoption of new or optimized processes.

6. Continuous Optimization & Evolution Cycle

Post initial phases, we provide ongoing monitoring, system updates, performance reviews, and expert consultations to ensure your Odoo system continually evolves with your business, keeping it efficient and competitive.

Ideal for:

SMEs seeking a manageable, phased approach to ERP implementation or optimization. Perfect for businesses wanting to avoid "big bang" projects, those with limited initial readiness, or those aiming for continuous, incremental improvements and quick wins from their ERP investment.
